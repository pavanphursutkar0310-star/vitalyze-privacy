Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 1, 2025  |  Last Updated: May 1, 2025  |  Version: 1.0

Welcome to Vitalyze AI ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

1.Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Account Information: Your name and email address when you create an account.
  • Health Data: Mood ratings, sleep hours, water intake, exercise duration, symptoms, and personal notes that you voluntarily log.
  • Food Photos: Images you upload for calorie and nutrition analysis. These are processed in real-time and are not stored permanently on our servers.
  • Daily Routine: Your custom health routine tasks and completion records.
  • Device Information: Basic device type and operating system for app compatibility purposes.

2.How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide and improve the Vitalyze AI experience:

  • To display your personal health dashboard and progress history.
  • To generate AI-powered health insights and recommendations tailored to you.
  • To analyze food photos and provide calorie and nutrition information.
  • To send health reminders and routine notifications (only if you enable them).
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs.
We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal health data with any third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

3.Data Storage & Security

Your health data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. Your account credentials are stored securely using industry-standard encryption.

  • All communication between the app and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • Food photos are processed in memory and are not saved to our servers after analysis.
  • AI chat messages are transmitted to our secure backend for processing and are not stored permanently.
  • We use Anthropic's Claude API for AI features. See Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy.

4.Camera & Photo Library Access

Vitalyze AI requests access to your camera and photo library only for the food calorie scanning feature. Specifically:

  • Camera access is used only when you tap "Scan Food" and choose to take a photo.
  • Photo library access is used only when you choose to upload an existing photo.
  • Photos are sent to our backend for AI analysis and are immediately discarded after results are returned.
  • We never access your camera or photos in the background.

5.Notifications

With your permission, we send push notifications to remind you of your health routine tasks. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings or within the app.

6.Children's Privacy

Vitalyze AI is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

7.Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all your health data within the app at any time.
  • Delete: Delete your account and all associated data from within the app ("Profile > Delete Account").
  • Export: Request a copy of your data by contacting us.
  • Opt-out: Disable notifications and AI features at any time.

8.Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Vitalyze AI:

  • Anthropic Claude API — for AI health coaching and food analysis. (Privacy Policy)
  • Expo / React Native — for app development infrastructure. (Privacy Policy)
  • Render.com — for secure backend hosting. (Privacy Policy)

9.Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, by sending a notification through the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10.Medical Disclaimer

Vitalyze AI is a wellness tool and is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of a qualified healthcare provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition.
